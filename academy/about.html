Our Story

Berean Academy was founded as a ministry of Berean Bible Baptist Church - Molino with a simple yet profound vision: to provide quality Christian education to families who desire it but cannot afford it.

Named after the Bereans of Acts 17:11 who "received the word with all readiness of mind, and searched the scriptures daily," our academy is committed to teaching students to think critically, study diligently, and live faithfully.

Since our opening, we have grown from a handful of students to a thriving community of learners, all united by our commitment to Christ-centered education.
