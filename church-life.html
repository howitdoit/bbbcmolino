Sunday School Programs

Our Sunday School provides age-appropriate biblical instruction for everyone in the family. Each class is designed to help believers grow in their understanding of Scripture and application to daily life.

  • Toddlers (Ages 0-5)
  • Juniors (Grades 1-6)
  • Youth Classes (Young People and Singles)
  • Adult Classes (Men & Ladies)

All classes meet every Sunday at 9:45 AM after the morning worship.

Prayer Meetings

Prayer is the foundation of our church life. We believe in the power of corporate prayer and make it a priority in everything we do.

  • Wednesday Evening Prayer Service
