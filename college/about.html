Our Vision

A generation of biblically trained leaders who faithfully proclaim God's Word, shepherd His church, and advance His kingdom through gospel-centered ministry.

Our Heritage

Established as a ministry of Berean Bible Baptist Church - Molino, our college continues the legacy of biblical training that began with the church's founding.

Named after the noble Bereans who "searched the Scriptures daily" (Acts 17:11), we are committed to producing graduates who know God's Word deeply and apply it faithfully.

Over the years, hundreds of students have passed through our doors, now serving as pastors, missionaries, church planters, and faithful lay leaders around the world.
