Our Daughter Churches

Church planting ministry - Multiplying disciples and establishing local churches

Multiplying Ministry Through Church Planting

BBBC Molino is committed to the Great Commission through church planting. We have been blessed to plant and support 8 daughter churches throughout the region, each serving their local communities with the Gospel of Jesus Christ. These churches maintain fellowship with the mother church while being autonomous in their local ministry.
